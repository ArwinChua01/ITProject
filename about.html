Imagineers Open-Source UI/UX Web Designs


The Imagineers group started from a very ambitious, yet determined web developer, Arwin David Chua. He is an aspiring front end web developer and a web designer. His hobby is creating web designs every time that opportunity smiles or inspiration struck his mind. Along with his brief journey, he encountered lots of web designers that made him to continuosly create web designs. On September 1 2018, he came up with a brilliant idea: CREATE A SINGLE WEBSITE FEAUTRING ALL THE BEST DESIGNS HE MADE ALONG WITH MANY DEVELOPERS AND DESIGNERS.


Imagineers group was formed in order to provide fellow web designers in ways of pre-made website templtes, themes, and frameworks. These designs are made for free as developers themselves still exerts the effort on manipulating the codes as they develop. Imagineers continue to dream and continue to create more and more web designs, templates, frameworks, and themes. Imagineers aim to create and produce moere dedicated and aspiring web designers and developers, just as the founder once became in his life.

Our Mission

The one and only mission of the Imagineers is to provide web designers and web developers the pre-made themes that they need for a faster and more reliable development period. These templates ultimately aims for a good development of websites that are designed for its users.
